== Description ==
DW Social Share is a free, lightweight plugin that will enable the social sharing for your WordPress Site. Include tons of style and support various of social networks, This plug-in will be the best transportation to transmit your information to the user.

Nowaday, Internet social network is the fastest way to reach the audiences, sharing is becoming more and more important to a website to promote their goods, sharing information, news….
DW Social Share provide very attractive button that will increase click rate of the visitor to your website.

= Feature: =
* Social sharing display in various of style: flat, horizontal, vertical (coming soon).
* Lightweight plugin.
* Support various of social sharing: Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus, Linkedin, Pinterest, Print, Email…
* Simple, handful and reliable.
* Count the number of shares and clicks (coming soon).
* Can arrange order of the social network (coming soon).
* Fully supported by [DesignWall](https://www.designwall.com)

== Installation ==

1. Open `wp-content/plugins` Folder
2. Put: `Folder: dw-social-share`
3. Activate `DW Social Share` Plugin
4. Go to `WP-Admin -> Settings -> DW Social Share` to configure the plugin.

= Usage =
1. Open `wp-content/themes/<YOUR THEME NAME>/index.php`
2. You may place it in archive.php, single.php, post.php or page.php also.
3. Find: `<?php while (have_posts()) : the_post(); ?>`
4. Add Anywhere Below It (The Place You Want The Views To Show): `<?php if( function_exists('dw_social_share') ) { dw_social_share(); } ?>`
5. Or you can use the shortcode `[dw_social_share]`
